Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the educational landscape, Krishna Academy began as a humble initiative by top academicians who realized the gap between school education and the rigorous demands of IIT-JEE and NEET.

Today, we have evolved into a premier institute renowned for our systematic, objective, and student-focused coaching model. We are driven by one purpose — empowering passionate learners to transcend their limits.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about admissions and our programs.

When do the new batches start?
New batches commence in April-May (summer), June-July (post-boards) each year. Dropper batches start from August-September.
Is there a scholarship or fee concession?
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships for students scoring above 80% in board exams and talent test qualifiers. Contact our admissions team for details.
Are online classes available?
We offer hybrid programs where classroom sessions are supplemented by our full-stack online portal with recorded lectures, live doubt sessions, and digital DPPs.
What is the batch size at Krishna Academy?
We cap batches at 35-40 students to ensure individual attention and meaningful faculty-student interaction.

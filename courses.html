IIT-JEE vs. NEET: Which is Right For You?

Choosing between Engineering and Medical paths defines your future. Let's break down what each exam demands.

IIT-JEE

Engineering path — IITs, NITs, IIITs & top engineering colleges. PCM subjects with deep analytical reasoning.

Engineering Track

NEET

Medical path — AIIMS, MBBS, BDS & top medical colleges. PCB subjects with NCERT mastery.

Medical Track
Criteria IIT-JEE (Main & Advanced) NEET (UG)
Primary Purpose Admission to IITs, NITs, IIITs & top engineering colleges Admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH medical courses
Core Subjects Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM) Physics, Chemistry, Biology — Botany & Zoology (PCB)
Exam Pattern Computer-Based Test (CBT). Main checks aptitude; Advanced tests deep conceptual logic. Pen & Paper OMR mode. High speed and accuracy are crucial.
Difficulty Focus Heavy analytical reasoning, application-based complex integrations. Voluminous syllabus recall, Biology memorization, rapid problem-solving.
Attempts 3 attempts for JEE Main; 2 for Advanced Unlimited attempts (as per NMC guidelines)
